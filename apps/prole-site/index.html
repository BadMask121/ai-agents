Installing Prole

Prole is ad-hoc signed (not yet notarized through the Apple Developer Program), so macOS Gatekeeper needs a one-time nudge on first launch. Here’s the whole flow:

  1. Open the download. Double-click Prole.dmg, then drag Prole into your Applications folder.
  2. First launch — right-click → Open. In Applications, right-click (or Control-click) Prole and choose Open. Confirm Open in the dialog. You only do this once.
    Prefer the terminal? Run: 
    xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Prole.app
    then open it normally.
  3. Grant Screen Recording. On your first capture, macOS asks for Screen Recording permission. Enable Prole in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording, then quit and reopen Prole if prompted.
