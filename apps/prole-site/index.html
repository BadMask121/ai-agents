How it works

  1. 1

    Snip it

    Hit your hotkey or click the menu bar icon, then draw a box around anything on screen.

  2. 2

    Mark it up

    Add arrows, boxes, or a freehand pen. Use the text tool to write your question right on the image.

  3. 3

    Paste it anywhere

    Hit Copy and the image is on your clipboard. Paste it into Claude, ChatGPT, Slack, email, or any app that takes an image.

Installing Prole

Prole isn’t notarized by Apple yet, so macOS needs a quick one-time OK the first time you open it. Here’s how.

  1. Open the download. Double-click Prole.dmg, then drag Prole into your Applications folder.
  2. Open it the first time. In Applications, right-click (or Control-click) Prole and choose Open, then confirm. You only do this once.
    Prefer the terminal? Run: 
    xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Prole.app
    then open it normally.
  3. Turn on Screen Recording. The first time you capture, macOS asks for permission. Enable Prole under System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording, then reopen Prole if it asks.
